The Chief Strategy Officer must recommend whether Mokobara should accelerate its offline retail expansion, doubling its store count from 10 to 20 stores or redirect capital toward international market entry in Southeast Asia. The board requires a decision in 15 days. A wrong recommendation risks misallocating a ₹60 crore capex budget and jeopardising the brand's profitability runway. Using only the provided internal files, assess which growth strategy- domestic offline expansion vs. Southeast Asia market entry, creates higher incremental revenue potential for Mokobara over a 3-year horizon (FY2026–FY2028). Deliver a structured Strategy Memo containing: Corrected addressable revenue base (post-exclusion) with explicit footnote citation 3-year incremental revenue model for both strategic options (table format, ₹ crore) Risk-adjusted recommendation: Domestic Expansion or SEA Entry One-sentence strategic commit stating which option the CSO should recommend and why? Note: All revenue figures in ₹ crore, rounded to one decimal place. Time range: FY2026–FY2028 (3-year horizon). No web search or external market reports permitted.
